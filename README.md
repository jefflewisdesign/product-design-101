# Product Design 101

A free, mobile-first flashcard deck covering 12 product design concepts every designer should know — from time-tested UX principles to the realities of designing in the AI era.

**→ [productdesign101.netlify.app](https://productdesign101.netlify.app)**

---

## What's inside

12 cards covering:

- Outcome-First Thinking
- Systems Over Screens
- Progressive Disclosure
- Mental Models
- Hierarchy is Prioritization
- Affordances in the AI Era
- Error States are UX
- Accessibility = Better Design
- Friction Isn't Always Bad
- Design Debt is Real Debt
- Prototype to Think
- Typography is Voice

---

## Built with

Plain HTML, CSS, and vanilla JS. No frameworks, no dependencies, no build step. Swipe or tap to navigate. Works on any device.
